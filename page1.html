DNA: The Code of Life

Background:

In order for DNA to be present in all new cells, it needs to replicate itself to make another identical copy. This is called DNA replication. In order for DNA to make proteins which is molecules all living organisms needs to fucntion, it needs to go throught transcription and translation. This process is called Protein Synthesis.
